About us

Our Mission

At Discord, our mission is to bring people together through the power of communication. We believe in creating a space where individuals can connect, share, and belong, regardless of who they are or where they come from. By providing a platform that fosters meaningful interactions and communities, we aim to break down barriers and enrich the lives of millions around the world.

What We Do?

Discord is a place where communities thrive. Whether you're a gamer, a creator, or simply someone looking to connect with like-minded individuals, Discord provides the tools you need to build and nurture communities that matter. From voice chats and video calls to text channels and customizable servers, our platform offers endless possibilities for collaboration, creativity, and camaraderie.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Jason Citron and Stanislav Vishnevskiy, Discord began as a solution to the shortcomings of existing communication tools for gamers. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a global phenomenon, attracting millions of users from all walks of life. Today, Discord is more than just a gaming platform—it's a home for diverse communities spanning gaming, art, music, education, and beyond.
